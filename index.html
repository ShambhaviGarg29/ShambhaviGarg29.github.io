My Story

I'm Shambhavi, a Class 12 student at Delhi Public School RK Puram. Growing up as an army kid, I attended 10 different schools across 10 years, living and learning across cities and cantonments all over India. That constant movement shaped me: it gave me adaptability, a sharp curiosity about the world, and a genuine love for meeting people from every walk of life.

I did my Class 10 from Army Public School, Bathinda, scoring 97%, before moving to DPS RK Puram for Class 11 and 12, where I scored 86.7% and 91% respectively. Through every school transition, I found ways not just to fit in, but to lead.

From publishing a policy report with the Government of India, to building India's first VR Combat Training Lab for the Indian Army, to winning a national gold medal for filmmaking, I try to bring rigor and creativity to everything I do. I am headed toward public policy, economics, or international relations, with a deep belief in using data and storytelling as tools for equity.
